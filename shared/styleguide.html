Hex motif

A recurring brand element inherited from the existing FirstCall identity. Used three ways: as a Markets-Served honeycomb on the homepage and branch sites, as a decorative anchor in section dividers and section-header eyebrows, and as a subtle background pattern on tinted bands.

Single hex — section anchor

A FirstCall Company

Industries We Serve honeycomb

A 19-hex concentric bullseye, two-tone. The inner cluster (darkest navy) is one hex at center plus a 6-hex ring — mission-critical specialty verticals, with Critical Infrastructure anchoring the center. The outer ring (mid navy, with a deeper hairline border to keep hex edges legible) is 12 hexes — broader commercial categories. Canonical source: shared/partials/industries-grid--main.html.

Hex pattern — subtle band background

A faint, repeating hex pattern that can dress up tinted bands without competing with content. Implemented as a CSS background.

Typography

Manrope for display / headings — geometric, premium, professional. IBM Plex Sans for body — humanist, slightly technical, highly readable. Both loaded from Google Fonts.
